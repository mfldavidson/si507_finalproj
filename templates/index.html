We the People Petitions by Issue Tag

About this App

This app extends the functionality of the White House's We the People Petitions site by allowing users to learn about currently open petitions as well as closed petitions by the issue with which they are tagged. It also displays visualizations to help users understand trends over time of petitions and signatures. Use the links above to navigate around the app. Learn more about the We the People site and petitions.

About the Petitions

The idea behind the We the People petitions site is that it allows people to create a petition and attempt to collect 100,000 signatures in 30 days. If the petition receives less than 100,000 signatures in 30 days, it is closed and no action is taken. If it receives 100,000 signatures or more in 30 days, the White House will issue an official response.
